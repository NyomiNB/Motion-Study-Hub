At Motion, we want you to be successful and stress-free. Here are some additional resources you can use beyond our website.

Inspirational Ted Talks:

Take a few minutes to listen to an inspirational speaker! They can help you gain a new perspective or motivate you to keep going.

Dan Pink's TED talk, "The Puzzle of Motivation," is a must-watch for students looking to unlock their true potential. In this engaging presentation, the bestselling author explores the latest research on motivation, debunking common myths about what drives us to succeed. Pink's insights will inspire you to rethink your approach to motivation and discover new ways to stay motivated and achieve your goals. Whether you're a student, an aspiring entrepreneur, or just seeking to boost your productivity, this talk offers valuable ideas you won't want to miss!

Azeez Lakhani's TEDx talk, "Do it for the 'Gram: The Age of Attention," offers a powerful look at how our obsession with social media is affecting our behavior and mental health. Lakhani's engaging presentation challenges students to rethink their digital habits and find a healthier balance online. If you're ready to break free from endless scrolling and use social media more mindfully, this talk is essential viewing. The blog also emphasizes the importance of mental health and the challenges Gen Z faces in the digital age.

Elevate your focus with Chris Bailey's TEDx talk, "How to Get Your Brain to Focus." This talk offers practical tips to help students cut through distractions and boost productivity. Whether you're managing online classes, assignments, or just want to get more done, Bailey's strategies will help sharpen your concentration. Tune in to discover effective ways to enhance your focus and tackle distractions head-on.
